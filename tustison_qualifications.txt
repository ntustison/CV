

Beyond Dr. Tustison's formal academic training, he has played an integral
developmental role in various well-known and open-source software frameworks for
well over 15 years. These include the Insight Toolkit where he has been one of
the top contributors of new algorithms, including the widely used N4 bias
correction algorithm and widely used registration framework.  He is also one of
the co-founders of the Advanced Normalization Tools (ANTs) library and its
various extensions, including the deep-learning ANTsRNet and ANTsPyNet packages.
